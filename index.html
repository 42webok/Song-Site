Bio & News

The Red Betty – TRB – is a Brazilian born, New Jersey based singer/songwriter who fuses her americana sound with the colors of South American percussion. Through her songwriting, TRB combines powerful, sweet, and nostalgic lyrics to unexpected instrumentation.
